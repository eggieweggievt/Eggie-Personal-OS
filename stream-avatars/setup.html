📖 How it all works

🖥️ Putting it in OBS

  1. Sources → + → Browser
  2. Paste your link in URL (don't tick "Local file")
  3. Width 1920 · Height 1080 — must match your canvas size, or avatars get squeezed into a corner
  4. FPS: Custom 60 (30 is fine too)
  5. Untick "Shutdown source when not visible"
Looks empty? Normal! Avatars appear when people chat. Use the Try it button's link (ends in &debug=1) while arranging your scene, then swap to the real one.

🎒 Gear & the viewer page

Gear: you upload PNG pieces into named sets above, and viewers equip with !{set} {piece} in chat (e.g. !items axe) — saved to their Twitch account, sticks across streams. There's also a gallery page: share picker.html?channel=yourname from this same site (needs the one-time Twitch app step in the README before sign-in works). Color skins are paused while the commissioned art gets sorted — the live preview shows a taste.

👑 Different sprites per role

In Use your own sprite above, the "Who uses this sprite?" menu lets you upload a separate look for Subscribers, VIPs, Mods, and the Broadcaster. Chatters automatically get the sprite of their highest role — a perk for subbing! Name labels also get a badge: 👑 🛡️ 💎 💜.

🧸 Sprite file tips

PNG animation sheet: all animation rows on one transparent sheet — rows are detected automatically and you map them: idle, walk, plus optional nap (!lurk) and jump rows. A 5-row sheet in the standard layout (idle/walk/sit/stand/jump) maps itself.
ZIP: frame images named like idle1.png, walk1.png, sit1.png, jump1.png — they're sorted into animations by name and stitched into a sheet.
Check the walking preview before saving; wrong row walking? Fix it with the dropdowns.

🔧 If something's weird

Avatars stuck in a small box: the OBS Width/Height don't match your canvas — fix the numbers in the source properties.
Changed something here but OBS shows the old version: right-click the source → Refresh cache of current page.
Nothing at all: open your link in a normal browser — if it 404s, the site needs a push (run update.ps1).
